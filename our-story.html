Our Heritage

Built In Karachi, Refined Through Years.

Since 2008, Zaiqa has served flavour-first cuisine built on family recipes and disciplined kitchen craft. Our identity is simple: warm hospitality, deep taste, and consistency in every service.

From One Kitchen

We began as a single local kitchen focused on authentic Pakistani classics and evolved into a full destination.

To A Culinary House

Today we host three cuisine lines, each with dedicated prep systems and chefs trained for consistency and quality.
